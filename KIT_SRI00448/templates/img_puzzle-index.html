EnigmaQuest: Puzzle Adventure
Get ready for a stimulating puzzle-solving experience! In this game, your objective is to rearrange shuffled puzzle pieces and unveil the complete image. The start screen allows you to choose from three levels of difficulty (1-3). Once selected, dive into the gameplay by clicking and swapping puzzle pieces to reconstruct the image. Successfully completing a level prompts you to press space to proceed or play again. Embrace the challenge, enjoy the vibrant visuals, and test your puzzle-solving skills!
